Fried Rice Recipe

Japanese Style Hibachi fried rice. A cozy style type of fried rice that makes you want more, even if you are on a "diet"!

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 cup chopped grilled chicken
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 2 tablespoons chopped garlic
  • 2 cups cooked white(or brown) rice
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  1. Step 1
  2. Gather All Ingredients
  3. Step 2
  4. Heat a wok or nonstick skillet over medium-high heat; add olive oil. Cook and stir eggs in the hot oil until cooked through, 2 to 3 minutes. Add chicken, green onion, and garlic to scrambled eggs; cook and stir until heated through, about 2 minutes.
  5. Step 3
  6. Mix rice into egg mixture; cook and stir until rice is warmed, about 2 minutes.
  7. Step 4
  8. Add soy sauce; cook and stir until rice mixture is evenly coated, about 3 minutes.
